Your Dish, Your Way Plan Your Week with Anyone Can Cook

Pick the recipes you want and customize for your life! Input your intolerances, diet type, or a type of dish you are looking for, like a main course or dessert. Alternatively, you can pick a particular cuisine or just tell us what you are hungry for! We'll do the heavy lifting and find some recipes you love!

You have full control over your week with our planner below, with access to recipes you've never even dreamed of! Use any or all search fields to review our recipes. Once you find something you like, click the checkbox next to the recipe and select the day you would like to make the dish, then click Add to My List! Once you've filled out your calendar, we will build your grocery list for you to review.

Step out of your comfort zone or make a classic! After you're done, you'll have a grocery list and a week of meals ready to go!
